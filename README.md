sudoku
======

Sudoku generator written in BASH

Output a random Sudoku on a simple textonly receipt printer:

```shell
./sudoku

         Sudoku
 +------+------+------+
 |  9   |     5 |  8  |
 |  5   |     7 |  6 3|
 |1 3 7 | 4 6 8 |2   9|
 +------+------+------+
 |  2 5 | 1   9 |  4  |
 |  7 9 |     4 |  3 1|
 |4     |   3 6 |  2 7|
 +------+------+------+
 |      | 9   2 |6   8|
 |5 6 8 | 7     |    2|
 |9 1   |   8 3 |  7 5|
 +------+------+------+


```
