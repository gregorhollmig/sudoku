sudoku
======

Sudoku generator written in BASH

Intention is outputting a Sudoku on a simple textonly receipt printer:
' ./sudoku
 +------+------+------+
 |2 5   |     6 |  1 9|
 |    6 | 5   1 |  4  |
 |  4 1 | 8   7 |6   3|
 +------+------+------+
 |1     |   5   |     |
 |4   9 | 3   2 |1 8  |
 |  8 3 | 7 1   |9 2 6|
 +------+------+------+
 |  1 8 |     5 |  9 2|
 |7     | 2 8   |    1|
 |6 2   | 1 7   |  3 8|
 +------+------+------+ '
